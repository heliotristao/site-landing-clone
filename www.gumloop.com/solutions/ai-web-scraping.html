Frequently asked questions

Still have questions? Reach out to our founders anytime.

Get in touch
What workflows can I automate with AI?

Repetitive, time-consuming tasks are the perfect candidates for automation. With Gumloop, you can build AI-driven workflows that fit your needs — allowing you to integrate your preferred LLM with existing tools in your marketing tech stack.

Why not use existing automation platforms?

Our automation builder is built with AI at the center. We don't aim to just streamline your workflow, but replace it entirely with modern AI technology.

Is it free?

We have a free plan! To unlock more powerful features and workflows, you will need to upgrade to one of our premium plans. You can check out our pricing here.

How do I get started?

There are many ways to get start with Gumloop! Check out the free courses at Gumloop University. Start with a template or from scratch, it's up to you!

What's your policy on file upload privacy?

All uploaded files are encrypted at rest and we have a strict policy on privacy. Read more about it here.

Can I collaborate with others on my flows?

Absolutely! You can invite other users to a Workspace or just send them the URL to your automation.

Where can I get technical support?

Join the Gumloop forum or email us at support@gumloop.com.
