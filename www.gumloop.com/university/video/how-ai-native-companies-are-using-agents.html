How AI-Native companies are using Agents

Thursday, January 29, 2026
,
10:00 am
Button Text
Watch now

Agents aren't just hype, they're running in production right now at companies like Samsara and Bright Harbor. But how did they actually get there?

​

In this panel, Marketing Ops Manager Shelby Belak (Samsara) and GTM Ops Manager Julia Carey (Bright Harbor) will share how they've put AI agents to work across their GTM stacks from lead operations to CRM management to real-time monitoring.

​

We'll explore:

  • Practical examples of how to use agents in your GTM stack
  • Best practices for bringing agents from idea to production
  • Strategies for getting buy-in and scaling adoption across your team
  • Lessons learned from real challenges they hit along the way

Whether you're in ops, marketing, sales, or just curious about what's actually possible with AI agents, you'll leave this session knowing exactly how to get started at your own company.
